## Become a Sponsor

If you have built a product that serves AI-assisted builders and want to support Riftbook, open an issue or reach out via GitHub.

Criteria:

- The product must be real and usable today.
- It must serve builders working with AI coding agents.
- No hype-only tools. No products without a practical workflow fit.
