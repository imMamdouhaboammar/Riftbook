**This path is for you if:** You are building a product, tool, or project alone — and you want to use coding agents as your team, not just as an autocomplete.

Solo builders have a specific challenge: you are the PM, the designer, the engineer, and the reviewer — all at once. Agents can help you cover all of those roles, but only if you have the right structure in place.

## The Solo Builder Reality

When you are building alone with agents, the biggest risk is not skill — it is judgment. Specifically:

- Knowing when agent output is good enough vs. when it needs correction
- Knowing when to keep building vs. when to stop and review
- Knowing when to push the lead agent vs. when to switch

This path is organized to give you judgment, not just technique.
