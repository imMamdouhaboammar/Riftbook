# Mistakes

This section exists because most resources only show what works. The gaps are where people actually get stuck.

## Why Mistakes Matter More Than Tips

A good tip gives you something to do.
A documented mistake gives you something to avoid.

Avoiding one bad pattern is often worth more than learning three new techniques — because bad patterns compound. They slow down projects in ways that are hard to diagnose after the fact.
