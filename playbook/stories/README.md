# Stories

**Real examples are easier to remember than abstract advice.**

This section contains short, honest accounts of real use. What happened, what I thought was happening, what actually mattered, and what I would do differently now.

These are not success stories. They are field notes.

## Why Stories Work

Facts tell you what to do.
Stories show you what it actually felt like when it went wrong.

When you read a story about someone switching agents too late, or overengineering a setup that never gets used, it sticks. The next time you start building, you remember it.

That is the point.
